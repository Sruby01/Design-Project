Why I chose this topic? I wanted to help students at St. Thomas because as a student trying to navigate my way around both the new school and the Covid times we're in, I wished there were more resources available to help.
What will you find on this site? You will be able to find maps of the St. Thomas building along with directions on how to get from class to class as easily and as quickly as possible. You will also find information about the cafeteria, the teachers and what you can do off campus.
